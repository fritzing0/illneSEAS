Anxiety

1/20 teens in the us has anxiety. Everyone feels anxious from time to time, but only diagnosed as anxiety disorder if it becomes excessive (if it is disproportionate to situation, disrupts life, distressing to person, or lasts long)

There are many different symptoms including lightheadedness, nausea, shortness of breath, chest pain, headaches, and sleep loss.

Talk to a trusted adult.
