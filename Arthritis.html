Arthritis

According to arthritis.org, arthritis is an informal way of referring to joint pain or joint disease with 100 different types of arthritis.

Symptoms can include pain, swelling, stiffness, decreased range of motion. Arthritis can affect you on different degrees and can change over time.

Support and resources can be found on https://www.arthritis.org/living-with-arthritis/tools-resources/
