Arthritis

https://www.arthritis.org/about-arthritis/understanding-arthritis/what-is-arthritis.php According to arthritis.org, arthritis is “an informal way of referring to joint pain or joint disease” with “100 different types of arthritis”. Symptoms can include pain, swelling, stiffness, decreased range of motion. Arthritis can affect you on different degrees and can change over time.
