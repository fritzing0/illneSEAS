Cerebral Palsy

According to cerbralpalsy.org, cerbral palsy is an “ umbrella term for a group of disorders that affects a person's ability to move. This means that not everyone with cerebral palsy looks the same” and diagnosis is usually between 0 to 5 months or age. Signs of cerebral palsy can include poor muscle control, prefers to use on side of the body, delayed development.
