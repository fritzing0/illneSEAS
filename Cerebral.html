Cerebral Palsy

According to cerbralpalsy.org, cerebral palsy is an “ umbrella term for a group of disorders that affects a person's ability to move. This means that not everyone with cerebral palsy looks the same” and diagnosis is usually between 0 to 5 months or age.

Signs of cerebral palsy can include poor muscle control, prefers to use on side of the body, delayed development.

Some support and resources can be found on http://kidshealth.org/en/parents/cerebral-palsy.html and also http://ucp.org
